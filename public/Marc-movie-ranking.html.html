The Best Movies According to Marc

My top 3 movies of all-time

#1. Wall Street (1987)

The exciting world of finance.

The ultimate pitfalls of power, intrigue and the allure of money.

#2. Margin Call (2011)

An insider's perspective of the mortgage bubble...

from the players that move the game.

#3. The Big Short (2015)

Expands on wallstreet's affect on real estate and the global economy.

Corruption at the highest levels of the corporate structure...

and everyone bathing in a fake bubble of wealth.
